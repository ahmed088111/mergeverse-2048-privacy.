Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Introduction

MergeVerse 2048 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. The app uses only local storage on your device to save your game progress, settings, and preferences.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to provide personalized ads. This may include your device's advertising ID, IP address, and usage data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Storage (SharedPreferences)

We use SharedPreferences to store the following data locally on your device:

  • Theme mode preference (light/dark)
  • Accent color selection
  • Board size setting
  • Sound and haptic preferences
  • Game state (tiles, score, undo history)
  • Game statistics and achievements

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Analytics

MergeVerse 2048 does not use any analytics services. We do not track your usage or collect any behavioral data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored locally on your device
  • Delete all app data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad privacy settings

Since we do not collect or store your data on external servers, no additional data deletion requests are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
